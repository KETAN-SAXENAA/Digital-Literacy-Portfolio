
Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness

In this task, I studied about phishing attacks.

Phishing is a type of cybercrime where attackers try to trick people into giving their personal information such as passwords or bank details through fake emails or messages.

Case Study:
A student received an email that looked like it was from a bank. The email asked for login details. After entering the details, the student lost money from the account.

Prevention:
- Always check the sender’s email address
- Do not click on unknown links
- Use strong passwords
- Enable two-factor authentication

Reflection:
This task helped me understand how cybercrimes happen and how we can protect ourselves from such attacks.
